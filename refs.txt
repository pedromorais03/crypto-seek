avg_energy_consumed_per_transaction 
BTC -> Average energy consumed per transaction output in kilowatt-hour (kWh) => Output_kWh 
ETH -> Average energy consumed per unit of gas in watt-hour (Wh) => Gas_unit_Wh 

carbon_footprint_per_transaction 
BTC -> Carbon footprint per transaction output in kilograms of CO2 (kgCO2) => Output_kgCO2 
ETH -> Carbon footprint per unit of gas in grams of CO2 (gCO2) => Gas_unit_gCO2
